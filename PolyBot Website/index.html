Meet PolyBot

PolyBot is a free Discord bot that aids students in their virtual language learning journey.

Features

Text Translations

With a simple command, you can understand and communicate in over 100+ languages

Text-to-Speech Translations

Using a text-to-speech platform, you can listen to how translated words are pronounced

Create quizzes

Explore our large database of language quizzes or customize your own quizzes to test your language skills

Be apart of the leaderboard

Collect points by answering quizzes and showcase yourself in the scoreboard
